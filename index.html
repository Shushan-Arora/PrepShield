About PrepShield

PrepShield is a comprehensive web app that helps users prepare for emergencies with essential plans, resources, and contact management tools. Its interactive widgets guide users through tailored preparedness plans, including tornado and hurricane responses, while providing easy access to critical contact information. With a user-friendly interface, PrepShield empowers individuals and families to stay informed and ready for any situation.
